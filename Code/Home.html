"Every child is an artist. The problem is
how to remain an artist once he grows up"
-Pablo Picasso

Students at Desert Hills High School have many opportunities to display their creativity, through digital and traditional artwork, and musical and vocal performance. Students at DHHS have performed at Major League Baseball Spring Training games, at talent shows and poetry festivals, and shown their works at Arizona Fan Fusion (formerly Arizona Comicon), among other venues. Please contact the DHHS front office for more information about our popular art and music programs.

We want to give students every chance to remain an artist.
